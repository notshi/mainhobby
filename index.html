Main Hobby play twisted indie pop songs built around the songwriting talent of Jem Emmett.

We played our debut gig at the legendary 1 in 12 club in Bradford with West Yorkshire Noblemen's Society. It was great and many people came to support fundraising efforts needed for building improvements to make the club more accessible.

More info about the 1 in 12 fundraiser can be found here.
