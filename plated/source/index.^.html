#^------------------------------ #^bandcamp trim=ends #^------------------------------ #^bio trim=ends form=markdown Main Hobby play twisted indie pop songs built around the songwriting talent of Jem Emmett. Based in Bradford, West Yorkshire, the band features shi Blank (*bass*), Helen Russell (*drums*), David Carpenter (*guitar*), and Jem Emmett (*vocals, guitar*). #^------------------------------ #^news trim=ends form=markdown We played our debut gig at the legendary 1 in 12 club with West Yorkshire Noblemen's Society. It was great and many people came to support fundraising efforts needed for building improvements to make the club more accessible. More info about the 1 in 12 fundraiser can be found [here](https://www.justgiving.com/crowdfunding/1in12-Refurbishment).
